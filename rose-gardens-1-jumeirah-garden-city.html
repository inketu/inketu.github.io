Live in Elegance: Rose Gardens 1 Residences in Dubai

Introduction

Rose Gardens 1 in Dubai’s Jumeirah Garden City redefines boutique luxury living. This exclusive residential project by Roz Real Estate combines contemporary architecture with thoughtfully designed apartments, offering a serene oasis in the midst of the city’s vibrant energy. With beautifully landscaped rooftop gardens, modern amenities, and a prime location near Dubai’s key landmarks, Rose Gardens 1 provides a perfect balance of comfort, style, and convenience for families, professionals, and investors alike.

Where Sophistication Blossoms

Nestled within the vibrant tapestry of Jumeirah Garden City, Rose Gardens 1 unfolds as more than just a residential development — it’s a lifestyle destination. This boutique gem from Roz Real Estate Development offers refined, connected, and charming urban living that’s both rooted in grace and elevated by innovation.

A Vision Brought to Life by Roz Real Estate

With a reputation for crafting spaces defined by precision and timeless elegance, Roz Real Estate Development infuses every project with a commitment to sustainable design and aesthetic excellence. >Rose Gardens 1 continues this legacy, blending innovation and enduring appeal in a boutique form.

Prime Positioning with Unmatched Connectivity

What truly sets Rose Gardens 1 apart is its prime location and seamless access to Dubai's most iconic realms — a testament to its thoughtfully curated setting:

  • Just 5 minutes from the Museum of the Future and City Walk
  • About 8 minutes to Palm Jumeirah and DIFC
  • Only 10 minutes from the heart of Downtown Dubai
  • A mere 15 minutes to Dubai International Airport (DXB)
  • Around 20 minutes to the ultra-luxurious Burj Al Arab

Amenities That Elevate Everyday Living

Rose Gardens 1 isn't just about where you live — it’s how you live:

  • A well-appointed Spacious Gym for fitness enthusiasts
  • Beautiful Rooftop Gardens offering tranquil, elevated green space
  • A Podium-Level Pool, ideal for leisure and social time
  • A vibrant Kids’ Play Area designed for family enjoyment

Residences That Reflect Your Style

From studios to 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom apartments, every unit at Rose Gardens 1 is designed with individuality and elegance in mind — giving you the flexibility to choose a space that truly suits your way of life.

Experience a Lifestyle Rooted in Charisma

This is more than a home — it’s a sanctuary where architecture meets serenity, and where every detail invites a modern lifestyle imbued with charm. From carefully planned amenities to serene green spaces, Rose Gardens 1 promises a living experience that’s both luxurious and authentically connected to Dubai’s dynamic pulse.
